Apollo Protocol

NFT fractionalization protocol, marketplace and AMM

Interact with our protocol to fractionalize your NFTs, whether it be part of a collection or a 1/1, and recieve fractions in the form of SPL tokens while your NFT is stored in our vault. Then these fractions can be traded via Apollo's inhouse P2P marketplace & AMM. If you wish, you can also earn passive income by providing liquidity to our AMM platform.

HEX Tools

Professional suite of NFT analytical and trading tools

HEX is a set of trading tools which allows a NFT trader to make the most well informed decision. Through the use of graphical and numerical solutions, both historical and real time data is presented in a way easily interpreted by the user. HEX also circumvents front-end technical limitations of marketplaces allowing for realtime sniping.
